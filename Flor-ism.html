Flor-ism

For families, friends, schools, and curious city wanderers.

Flor-ism is a guided plant quest in the park of Issy-les-Moulineaux. Explore, identify and stamp your way through a curated route of local species while learning how everyday plants keep the city alive.

How it works

Plant quest · Stamp card · Rewards

During Flor-ism, the park becomes a mapped field guide. Every checkpoint introduces one plant, one story, and one simple action you can take to protect urban ecosystems.

1
Collect your plant passport
At the welcome point, receive a map and a Flor-ism stamp card. Each empty circle represents one plant you can find.
2
Follow the trail
Walk through the park to locate highlighted trees, shrubs, herbs, and ground plants. Clear markers and simple clues guide you.
3
Stamp & learn
At each plant station, stamp your card and read concise panels about pollinators, soil, water cycles, and how this species supports the city.
4
Reach reward tiers
Return to the welcome point to redeem eco-themed rewards depending on how many plants you found.
